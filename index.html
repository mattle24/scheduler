Hard Constraints (always enforced)

  • No team plays twice on the same day
  • No team plays 3+ consecutive calendar days
  • In the first 10 days, no team plays within 2 days of each other
  • In the last 5 days, each team plays at most 1 game
  • AL/NL: each team plays every intra-league opponent at least once
